Core features

Everything your AI agent does for you

From photo upload to completed sale — VintAgent handles the entire workflow automatically.

Auto-listing & cross-posting

Upload photos once. VintAgent creates and publishes listings on Vinted and Vestiaire simultaneously, with platform-optimised formatting.

AI price optimisation

The agent scans real-time market data for comparable items and suggests the optimal price to maximise revenue and sell-through speed.

AI description writing

GPT-powered descriptions in English and French, tailored to each platform's tone — detailed for Vestiaire, casual for Vinted.

Smart offer management

Set your minimum price thresholds. The agent auto-accepts, declines, or counters offers based on your rules — even at 3am.

Background removal & styling

AI automatically removes cluttered backgrounds from your photos and adds clean white or lifestyle-style backgrounds for higher conversion.

Performance analytics

Track your listings across both platforms in one dashboard. See views, saves, offers received, and revenue — all in real time.

    FAQ

    Common questions

    Does VintAgent require access to my Vinted password?
    ▼
    No. VintAgent connects via OAuth — the same secure standard used by "Sign in with Google". You never share your password with us.
    Will using a bot violate Vinted's terms of service?
    ▼
    VintAgent acts as an assistant, not an automated bot. Listings are reviewed and approved by you before going live. Our approach mirrors tools like Buffer for social media — widely used and accepted by platforms.
    Which languages does the AI write descriptions in?
    ▼
    Currently English and French. German and Dutch support is coming in Q2. You can also set your preferred language per platform.
    How accurate is the brand and condition detection?
    ▼
    Our vision model correctly identifies brand and category in ~90% of cases for common brands. You can always correct errors before publishing — the AI output is always editable.
    Can I cancel anytime?
    ▼
    Yes. Cancel any time with one click, no questions asked. Your listings remain live after cancellation — we don't delete anything.
